# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis 2022

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the 2022 sales data of **Vrinda Store**, an online clothing retailer, to understand customer purchasing behavior and overall sales performance.

The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that can help the company make data-driven decisions and improve sales growth in 2023.

## Business Objective

Vrinda Store wants to create an annual sales report for 2022 to better understand its customers and identify opportunities to increase sales in 2023.

## Tools & Skills Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning
- Data Processing
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Dashboard Design
- Data Visualization

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Corrected inconsistent entries
- Standardized data formatting
- Verified data quality

### 2. Data Processing
- Created additional business columns
- Grouped customers into age categories
- Prepared data for reporting and visualization

### 3. Data Analysis
Using Pivot Tables, the data was analyzed to understand:

- Sales by gender
- Sales by age group
- State-wise sales
- Sales by channel
- Order status
- Monthly sales performance

### 4. Dashboard Development

An interactive Excel dashboard was created using:

- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers

The dashboard allows users to dynamically filter and analyze sales across different business dimensions.

## Dashboard Insights

- Women contributed approximately **64%** of total sales.
- The **Adult** age group generated the highest sales.
- **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** were the top-performing states.
- **Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart** were the leading sales channels.
- More than **90%** of orders were successfully delivered.
- Monthly sales trends revealed seasonal variations in customer demand.
  
## Business Recommendation

Based on the analysis, Vrinda Store can improve sales by:

- Targeting adult women customers through personalized marketing campaigns.
- Increasing promotional activities in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.
- Strengthening partnerships with Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart.
- Leveraging seasonal sales trends to optimize inventory and marketing strategies.
